Zero'G'ames Kit

This kit is the perfect way to present and enjoy a variety of games

MAGNETIC DICE

Aim True, Stick with Magnetic Precision!

"Magnetic Dice" is an interactive, space-themed dice game that combines elements of strategy, luck, and fun. Players navigate through various galactic scenarios, using magnetic dice to influence the outcomes of their adventures. The game can be played with friends or solo and encourages creativity and decision-making.

SPIN A BOTTLE

Spin the Bottle, Unveil the Cosmos: Adventure is Just a Twist Away!

"The Aluminum Bottle Spin Game engages astronauts in microgravity by having them spin a bottle, which stops via a reaction wheel, with a laser pointer selecting the chosen player."

MAG x DART

"Defy Gravity, Hit the Bullseye: Precision Meets Play in Every Throw!"

The Magnetic Dart Game, included in the Zero'G'ames kit, offers astronauts a fun activity in microgravity. Players throw magnetic darts at a specially designed board that holds the darts securely upon contact. With various scoring sections, it encourages precision and friendly competition. This game is an engaging way to unwind and connect with fellow astronauts while floating in space!

About DarkNova

This website was created by a group of six enthusiastic Computer Science students from MES College of Engineering Kuttippuram — Adarsh, Hisham, Athul, Ahmed, Mirza, and Jasir, A, the website developer — as part of our hackathon project. We share a deep fascination with space and technology, which inspired us to design this platform dedicated to exploring the wonders of the universe. Together, we've combined our skills and passion to bring space closer to everyone. We hope this project sparks the same curiosity and excitement about the cosmos that drives us!
