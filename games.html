GAME DETAILS

MAGNETIC DICE

REQUIREMENTS:

1. Storage

2. Game Board

How To Play:

1. Roll the dice by giving them a gentle push and a spin.

2. The magnets will help them stay in place while reaching the magnetic surface of the Zero'G'ames Kit Box.

3. Use the dice for various games (e.g., board games, dice games) or create your own.

4. Keep score as per the rules of the chosen game.

SPIN A BOTTLE

REQUIREMENTS:

1. Storage

2. Battery backup

How To Play:

Set Up: Ensure a clear area and gather all players for the game.

Spin the Bottle: One player gives the bottle a strong spin to start the game.

Timer and Stop: A timer activates, randomly expiring within 2 to 3 seconds, after which the reaction wheel stops the bottle.

Select a Player: A laser pointer activates to select the player the bottle points to, who then completes a challenge or task.

MAG x DART

REQUIREMENTS:

1. Storage

2. Magnetic Board

How To Play:

1. Players throw magnetic darts that travels a short distance away from the target.

2.Darts stick to the board,aiming for various scoring sections.
